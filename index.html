iOS App Coming Soon

Our iOS app will be available very soon on the App Store. In the meantime, you can add our website to your home screen for the best experience.

How to Add to Home Screen:

For Safari (iPhone/iPad):

  1. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Tap "Add" in the top right corner
  4. The Bondly app will appear on your home screen

For Chrome (Android/iPhone):

  1. Tap the three dots menu in the top right
  2. Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"
  3. Confirm by tapping "Add" or "Install"
  4. The Bondly app will be installed on your home screen
