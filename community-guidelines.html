Community Guidelines

These guidelines help ensure Bondly remains a safe and welcoming space for everyone.

At Bondly, we're committed to creating a respectful, safe, and inclusive environment for all members. These Community Guidelines outline our expectations for member behavior and help ensure everyone has a positive experience.

1. Be Respectful

Treat all members with kindness and respect. We celebrate diversity and expect all members to be considerate of others' backgrounds, beliefs, and identities.

Do:

  • Use polite and respectful language
  • Be open to different perspectives
  • Respect personal boundaries
  • Report any inappropriate behavior

Don't:

  • Use hate speech or slurs
  • Bully, harass, or intimidate others
  • Make unwanted sexual advances
  • Discriminate based on any characteristic

2. Authentic Profiles

We require all members to maintain accurate, up-to-date profiles that represent themselves authentically.

  • Use recent photos that clearly show your face
  • Provide truthful information about yourself
  • Don't create accounts for other people
  • Don't use fake names or impersonate others

3. Appropriate Content

All content shared on LoveConnect must be appropriate for our dating community.

  • No nudity or sexually explicit content
  • No violent, graphic, or disturbing content
  • No promotion of illegal activities
  • No spam, advertisements, or commercial content

4. Safe Interactions

Your safety is our priority. Please follow these guidelines when interacting with others:

  • Don't share personal contact information too quickly
  • Be cautious when sharing financial information
  • Report any suspicious behavior immediately
  • Meet in public places for first dates

5. Age Requirements

Bondly is for adults aged 18 and over. We strictly prohibit:

  • Underage users on the platform
  • Any content that sexualizes minors
  • Requests for age-inappropriate relationships

6. Consequences for Violations

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

  • Content removal
  • Temporary account suspension
  • Permanent account termination
  • Legal action in severe cases

7. Reporting Issues

Help us maintain a safe community by reporting any violations or concerns:

  • Use the "Report" feature on profiles or messages
  • Email us at safety@Bondly.com
  • Include screenshots when reporting specific incidents

We regularly review and update these guidelines to ensure the safety and quality of our community. By using Bondly, you agree to follow these rules.
